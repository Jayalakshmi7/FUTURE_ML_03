# Customer Support Chatbot Demo

This is a demo conversation of a professional customer support chatbot using Dialogflow & Telegram.

## Sample Conversation

**Bot:** Welcome to our Support Bot, how can I assist you?  

**User:** I can’t login  
**Bot:** Please use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password.  
If the problem persists, contact our support team at support@example.com.  

**User:** Where is my order?  
**Bot:** You can track your order in the "My Orders" section.  
If you need further assistance, contact support at support@example.com.  

**User:** How do I get a refund?  
**Bot:** Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.  
You will receive a confirmation email once it’s completed.  

**User:** How can I contact support?  
**Bot:** You can contact support via email at support@example.com or call 1800-123-456.  
Our team is happy to assist you.  

**User:** random text  
**Bot:** Sorry, I didn't understand that. Please contact support at support@example.com.  
I’m here to help with orders, account issues, or technical problems.
